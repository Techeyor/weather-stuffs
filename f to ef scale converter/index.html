The Fujita to Enhanced Fujita Scale Wind Speed Converter

For context, the estimated windspeeds on the Fuijta scale beyond F3 are overestimated. So when the National Weather Service created the Enhanced Fujita scale, they used a complex equation to convert those overestiated winds to more accurate guesses. This is why F4 and F5 are so different compared to EF4 and EF5. With this method, it is possible to convert F6-12 to the EF scale, which I have converted the F6 to the Enhanced Scale for you.
